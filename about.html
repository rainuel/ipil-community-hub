About Ipil Community Hub

A student-led initiative designed to connect residents of Ipil with local news, services, and opportunities.

Our Mission

To make community information accessible, up-to-date, and useful for every resident of Ipil. By centralizing local updates, services, and events, we aim to strengthen community connection.

Who We Are

The Ipil Community Hub was created by a close-knit group of friends who share a passion for technology, public service, and uplifting our local community. We believe that with the right digital tools, we can make information more accessible and empower our neighbors to stay connected and informed.
