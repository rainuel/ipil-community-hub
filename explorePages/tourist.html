Tourist Spots

Discover iconic landmarks and attractions in Ipil.

Rotunda

Ipil’s Rotunda Obelisk is a central, tall landmark at the town center of Ipil, Zamboanga Sibugay, serving as a memorial and symbol of regional identity with a fountain and surrounding greenery.

Zamboanga Sibugay Capitol

The capitol area sits on a hill in Ipil, offering prominent views and serving as the province’s administrative hub.

Buluan Island

Buluan Island is a small, tranquil island off Ipil in Zamboanga Sibugay, known for its white-sand beaches, clear turquoise waters, and relatively undeveloped, secluded shorelines.
